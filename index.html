Women safety App

Domestic violence is a pervasive issue that affects individuals regardless of gender, but its impact is often more severe on women and marginalized groups. A gender-responsive mechanism aims to address this imbalance by creating targeted strategies that account for the unique challenges faced by these groups. Such mechanisms prioritize understanding the root causes of domestic violence through a gender lens, focusing on prevention, protection, and empowerment. This approach includes creating safe spaces, offering tailored support services, enacting stronger legal protections, and raising public awareness about gender-based violence. By adopting a gender-responsive approach, communities and policymakers can work towards a more equitable society, where survivors are supported, and systemic violence is actively dismantled.
